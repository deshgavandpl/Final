League Information

About the League

The Deshgavhan Premier League is a community-driven, highly competitive cricket tournament aimed at discovering and showcasing local talent. Operating on a professional franchise model.

Vision & Mission

To provide a state-of-the-art platform for Deshgavhan's youth, promoting sportsmanship, unity, and physical excellence through the spirit of cricket.

Tournament History

Season 1

The inaugural season that started it all. Winners: AP11

Season 2

Expanded format with new franchises. Winners: Cotton11

Structure & Rules

  • 1.
    Match Format: Strictly T10 (10 Overs per inning). 90-minute match duration.
  • 2.
    Player Eligibility: MUST BE DESHGAVHAN DOMICILE. Outside players are strictly prohibited.
  • 3.
    Auction: All teams have an equal virtual purse. Base prices determined by committee.
